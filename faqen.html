Questions & Answers

Everything you need to know before we start your project.

Is the filming legal?

Yes. Every flight by Sky Studio M is fully licensed and compliant with CAA and EASA regulations. We handle all necessary permits for flights in residential areas or national parks.

How long does editing take?

It depends on the package. SOCIAL PRO videos are ready within 48 hours, while CINEMATIC PREMIUM takes 3-5 business days due to advanced DaVinci Resolve color grading.

What if the weather is bad?

Safety is our priority. In case of high winds or rain, the session is rescheduled to the first available time that suits you, at no extra cost.

Do you provide raw footage?

Yes, if you choose the BASIC RAW package, you receive all unedited 4K shots directly via Cloud storage.
