Who are we?

Sky Studio M is a professional aerial cinematography and video production studio, born from the fusion of long-term corporate management and a passion for cutting-edge technology. With over 14 years of business experience in sales and management, we don't just offer footage — we provide a strategic approach to visual communications. We understand that every project has a business goal; therefore, our process is focused on precision, accountability, and premium quality.

Your B2B Partner: Sky Studio M

Positioned as a reliable collaborator for: Video Productions & Marketing Agencies (providing professional outsourcing/subcontracting services with an EASA certified pilot, ensuring full legality and safety). Real Estate & Corporate Projects: Creating visual presentations that highlight the quality and true value of your assets and brands.

Technical Capabilities & Post-production

We invest in the latest technology to ensure uncompromising quality: Advanced Aerial Filming using DJI systems for 4K RAW and D-Log formats. Digital Processing via DaVinci Resolve on the latest Apple M4 platform, guaranteeing rapid delivery and high-end cinematic aesthetics.
